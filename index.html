Passive Events

Tech&Meet: The Quantum Threat – Are You Ready?

Attended this Tech&Meet session on March 25, 2025, where Eric Michiels, Executive Architect at IBM and IBM Quantum Technical Ambassador, presented on quantum computing security challenges. The session showcased IBM's quantum computing platform with various QPUs (Quantum Processing Units), the differences between classical and quantum computing architectures, and NIST's quantum-safe cryptography standards such as CRYSTALS-Kyber and CRYSTALS-DILITHIUM. Eric demonstrated real-world Qiskit implementations and emphasized the urgency of migrating to quantum-resistant algorithms before quantum computers can break current cryptographic systems. Particularly concerning was the warning that quantum computers capable of breaking 2048-bit RSA could be available by 2030, highlighting the "harvest now, decrypt later" threat model.
